A B O U T

Our company was founded by Will and Will to develop a wireless security testing device modeled after the Hak5 Pineapple. It all started when Will met Will when Will was on a walk to Goodwill. After Will bumped into Will on the way to Goodwill, Will presented Will with a great business idea. The idea branched off from Will, a cyber security analyst, who had a terrible experience with his wifi auditing device not having enough range to conduct proper penetration tests. Therefore, Will & Will Security strives to develop a wifi auditing device with range better than a Hak5 Wifi Pineapple.
